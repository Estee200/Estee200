## 🚀 About Me 
I am a Data Analyst who loves finding the story behind the “flying numbers” and transforming raw data into clarity, truth, and direction.

With every dataset I touch, my goal is simple:
to bring openness, transparency, and meaningful insight to information that feels scattered or hidden.

My background as a Chartered Accountant has shaped my analytical instincts and sharpened my speed in uncovering patterns. It gives me a strong foundation in financial logic, accuracy, and audit-level attention to detail... truly, “I know every beat that hides in the numbers.”

Over time, I’ve worked on projects across:

Finance

Health

Sales & Retail

Personal and exploratory analytics

Each project has strengthened my ability to model data, visualize insights, communicate findings, and support better decision-making.

Today, I’m committed to continuous learning, building impactful dashboards, and helping businesses interpret their data with confidence.
